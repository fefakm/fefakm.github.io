Rehabilitation

We believe that pets should not have limited options when it comes to pain management and rehabilitation.

We have specialized, state-of-the-art equipment availiable for treating all of our adopted special needs pets. We pride ourselves in providing affordable care and develop an individualized rehabilitation therapy plans to provide better quality of life to every pet brought to us.

Training & Counseling

Adopting a special needs pet can bring challenges to their new forever family. It might also take a bit of time for the animal to get used to its new environment. Thinking about this, we offer counseling sessions with especialized professionals to help the transition.

We also offer different training programs according to each animal's needs: behaviour correction, anxiety and fear, etc. We focus on positive reinforcement and never punishments. This will empower you as the owner to be an integral part of the process
